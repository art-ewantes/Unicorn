How can my company be nominated?

If you are an Asian start up and believe your company has the potential to become a Unicorn in the next six years , there are two ways in which you can be nominated for this prestigious “I Am Future” Unicorn Award.

PERSONAL NOMINATION

You, yourself, along with people in your employ can nominate your company as a potential Future Unicorn. The more nominations your company receives, the better chance you have of making a favourable impression on the judges. Personal voters get to spin the wheel of fortune on the website for instant prizes, just to thank you for taking the time to vote.
